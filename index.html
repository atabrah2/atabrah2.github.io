Barney's Best Lines

Challenge Accepted

Whenever one of the gang or anyone else nearby Barney says that something is impossible, Barney takes up the challenge to disprove them. He has taken on serious challenges like cheering up Robin to the most ridiculous challenges like getting a girl's number while talking like a dolphin.

True Story

Barney has a tendency of saying "true story" after something that he doesn't really mean to be true, whether it's just something that he wants someone else to believe or a fake history lesson that he gives to Ted.

Awesome

Barney thinks that anything he does is awesome and that's obvously a fact. He says, "When I get sad I stop being sad and be awesome instead... True story." In another instance, he says, "Where my sadness gland should be there is only a second awesome gland.... True story."

Self five

Whenever Barney says something that he thinks is an awesome retort or joke, he gives himself a high five. Because he is the only one that understands his own awesomeness, he has to be the first one to give himself a high five.

Bro___

Barney has the tendency to add bro into every single word in which it could be applicable because he strictly adheres to The Bro Code. For example, he turns "Row, Row, Row Your Boat" into " Bro, Bro, Bro Your Broat" and changes the remaining lyrics to his own tastes.

Suit up!

Barney's love for suits is unparalleled. Whenever he doesn't see Ted wearing a suit and thinks that he should be wearing one, he calls him out. It is very rare to see Barney in anything but a suit, and if he isn't wearing an actual suit, he will be wearing a penguin "suit" or his birthday "suit".

Legen... wait for it... dary!

The most famous of Barney's quotes and my personal favorite, Barney pulls this line out all the time as a greater version of "awesome". He always uses it to describe just how amazing and dramatic an event is going to be. His best line is, " It's going to be legen-- wait for it-- and I hope you're not lactose intolerant, because the second half of the word is-- dary! Legendary!"
